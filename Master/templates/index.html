An elected 18-person selection committee votes on Hall of Fame induction. Have they been correct?

This site uses Machine Learning techniques to predict which NHL players should be in the Hockey Hall of Fame in Toronto.

Enter a player in the search bar above to see the results of our analysis.

We performed our analysis using the following machine learning models: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost. For our training set, we looked at players that played from 1967 to 2017 with more than 100 games played. We also left goalies out of our dataset to allow us to compare the same stats for every player. Forwards are represented with POS = 1 and defenders are POS = 2.
